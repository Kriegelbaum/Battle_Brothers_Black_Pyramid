Dual Weapon System
⚔️
Two-Weapon Fighter BP Augmenteds

After any mainhand melee attack resolves, 15% chance to immediately follow up with the offhand weapon's basic attack. The follow-up costs Fatigue but no AP. Activates when a one-handed melee weapon occupies the Offhand slot — not a shield, not a net, not a two-handed or ranged weapon.

With both hands empty, you fight as a brawler: every basic attack is followed by a second unarmed strike with 100% chance.

Carried by
Brigand Duelist
Brigand Blade Dancer
Brigand Blademaster
Brigand Ironbreaker (60% dual)
Nomad Dervish
🌀
Florentine Stance BP Augmenteds

While Two-Weapon Fighter is active and the offhand slot holds a weapon: gain +Melee Defense equal to 12% of current Resolve, capped at +15, and raise the free-strike chance from 15% to 20%. With Resolve 95 the bonus is roughly +11 Melee Defense on top of base.

With both hands empty, the brawler follow-up gains a 50% chance to leave the target Dazed.

Carried by
Brigand Blademaster only
Initiative-Based Perks
💨
Quick BP Augmenteds

Increases maximum Initiative by +10% (applied before fatigue penalties). Desert Hawk: 138 → ~152. Nomad Dervish: 155 → ~170. Both act before the majority of a player's front line.

Carried by
Nomad Desert Hawk
Nomad Dervish
🗡️
Poke and Kite BP Augmenteds

Every attack that costs AP gains flat bonus damage equal to 8% of current Initiative, capped at +15. Applies to both throws and melee swings. Examples: Skirmisher Init 120 → +9; Sand Stalker at night Init ~178 → +14; Axe Hurler Init 128 → +10 per throw.

Carried by
Brigand Skirmisher
Nomad Sand Stalker
Axe Hurler
Darkness & Range Perks
🌑
Shadow Reaper BP Augmenteds

While fighting at night or underground: all of Melee Skill, Ranged Skill, Initiative, and Resolve gain +10%, and Fatigue Recovery gains +3 per turn. Sand Stalker at night reaches Initiative ~178, acting before virtually every brother. Stacks with Backstabber for flanking hits.

Carried by
Nomad Sand Stalker
🏹
Close Combat Archer BP Augmenteds

Removes the penalty for using a bow while engaged in melee (the "can't shoot while adjacent to an enemy" restriction). Applies to bows only — not crossbows. The Desert Hawk is the only enemy in the game who stands in melee range and keeps shooting.

Carried by
Nomad Desert Hawk only
Anti-Armour Perks
🔩
Woe of Iron BP Augmenteds

On landing a melee hit, deals bonus armour damage equal to 8% of the target's current body/head armour condition. No cap. The heavier and less damaged the armour, the harder this barbarian dents it — a fresh suit of plate loses ~22-30 condition per hit on top of the regular damage. Designed to chew through Battle Forged brothers from the start.

Target armour HP Bonus armour damage Example

Carried by
Northern Ironbreaker
Brigand Ironbreaker
🩸
Woe of Flesh BP Augmenteds

Companion to Woe of Iron. On landing a melee hit, deals bonus HP damage that bypasses armour entirely, equal to 7% of the target's current body/head armour condition, capped at +20 HP. Where Woe of Iron strips the plate, Woe of Flesh punches through it. Same input scaling — the heavier the armour on target, the bigger the bonus — but the damage goes straight to the meat.

Note: this is the bonus that Woe of Iron used to apply (HP-bypass) before that perk was retasked to armour damage. Both perks now coexist, and an enemy carrying both will degrade the target's armour and punch HP damage through it on the same swing.

Carried by
Northern Ironbreaker
Brigand Ironbreaker
Combat Mastery
Ahead of the Curve BP Augmenteds

Gain 10% of current Initiative as bonus to both Melee Skill and Ranged Skill, capped at +20 each. The bonus scales with combat Initiative — it drops as fatigue accumulates. Veteran Init 105 → +10 MS/RS. Crossbowman uses this to improve ranged accuracy late in battle.

Carried by
Brigand Veteran
Brigand Crossbowman
Nomad Desert Hawk
Doctrine of Endurance BP Augmenteds

Gain 12% of current Resolve as damage reduction to both HP and Armour received, capped at 90% total reduction. Also grants immunity to Stun. Deserter Resolve 100 → −12% all incoming damage. Captain Resolve 120 → −14%. Makes both nearly impossible to burst down.

Carried by
Brigand Deserter
Brigand Captain
Martial Arts Master

A self-contained perk branch on a custom SpecialPerkGroup (pg.bp_aug.martial_arts_master). All four perks share one ironclad gate: no body armor and no helmet. The three advanced perks are also locked behind Monastery Training until that perk has been picked. Energy of Qi additionally grants the active skill Laying on Hands. Background access (rolled once at character generation): Monk 75%, Brawler 35%, Gladiator (any) 25%, Flagellant 20%.

🧘
Monastery Training BP Augmenteds

Tier 3 — gate of the branch. While unarmored, gain 25% of current Resolve as Melee Defense and 15% of current Initiative as Ranged Defense. Resolve 130 / Initiative 110 → +32 MD, +16 RD. Without it the next three perks stay locked.

Carried by
Player only — Monk 75%, Brawler 35%, Gladiator 25%, Flagellant 20%
👻
Ghost Step BP Augmenteds

Tier 5, requires Monastery Training. While unarmored: Rotation and Footwork cost 0 Fatigue / 2 AP instead of vanilla 25/4 and 15/3 — these are the perk's main value, since exiting Zone of Control no longer provokes a free attack on cheap movement. Also gain 5% of current Initiative as both Melee and Ranged Defense (small kicker on top of Monastery Training).

Carried by
Player only — same backgrounds as Monastery Training
✨
Spiritual Armor BP Augmenteds

Tier 6, requires Monastery Training. While unarmored, all incoming damage is reduced by a percentage of current Resolve via a two-anchor curve.

Resolve Damage reduction Note
5042.5%low Resolve, half effect
10085%first anchor
13088.75%typical late-game monk
180+95% (cap)second anchor / hard cap

Functionally a Nimble that requires no armor at all — the body is the buffer, the spirit is the absorber. Note: the perk has no armor to fall back on; once Resolve drops (Charm, low morale, fear), the reduction drops with it.

Carried by
Player only — same backgrounds as Monastery Training
☯️
Energy of Qi BP Augmenteds

Tier 7, requires Monastery Training. While unarmored, every melee hit deals bonus HP damage that bypasses armor, equal to 10% of (current Resolve + remaining stamina), capped at +30 HP per swing. Remaining stamina = MaxFatigue − CurrentFatigue, so the monk hits hardest when fresh and weakens as he tires. He must rest to restore power.

State Bonus HP damage Example
Fresh+20Resolve 100, MaxFat 100, CurFat 0
Half-spent+15Resolve 100, CurFat 50
Exhausted+10Resolve 100, CurFat ≥ MaxFat
Late-game peak+26 → +30 (cap)Resolve 130, MaxFat 130, fresh

Also grants the active skill Laying on Hands (5 AP / 15 Fatigue, range 1): heals an adjacent ally for 10% of their max HP and removes all poisons, bleeds, and charm effects (incl. Hexe). Self-target intentionally disabled for balance — Spiritual Armor already provides massive damage reduction; an in-combat self-heal on top would make the monk nearly unkillable.

Carried by
Player only — same backgrounds as Monastery Training
